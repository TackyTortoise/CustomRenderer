Materials are encapsulated by <Name></Name>
You can create materials with these properties (case and spacing insensitive)
	Color
	Transparency
	Reflectivity
	Roughness
	RefractiveIndex
	Shininess
	IsMetallic
	Texture
	TextureScale
	NormalMap
	ReflectivityMap
	TransparencyMap
	sRGBTexture
	
All textures are loaded from the Data/Textures folder
These materials work with instancing, so if one is adjusted, all are

<TestMaterial>
Texture = test.jpg
Reflectivity = 0.75
</TestMaterial>
